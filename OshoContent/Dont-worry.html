Live in the present

Here, Osho describes these three aspects of life with profound insight and wisdom.

Live in the Present:
Osho emphasizes the importance of living in the present moment, free from the burdens of the past and the anxieties of the future. He teaches that true happiness can only be found in the here and now, and that by fully immersing ourselves in the present, we can experience a profound sense of peace and contentment.

Creativity :
According to Osho, creativity is not limited to artistic expression; it is a way of life. Do something that makes you happy. Even if that things has no value. like someone drawing, someone singing. Do something that just makes you happy, but has no inner value. creativity is doing something without expectation.

Non-Seriousness:
Osho's concept of non-seriousness is not about being frivolous or irresponsible; it is about approaching life with a light heart and a playful attitude. He suggests that we should not take ourselves too seriously, as this can lead to unnecessary stress and suffering. Instead, Osho encourages us to embrace a sense of humor and lightheartedness, allowing us to navigate life's challenges with grace and ease.

In summary, Osho's teachings on living in the present, creativity, and non-seriousness offer profound insights into how we can live more fulfilling and joyful lives. He reminds us that life is a precious gift, and that by embracing these principles, we can unlock our true potential and experience a deeper sense of peace and happiness.
